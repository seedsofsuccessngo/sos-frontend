Be Part of the Journey

Our goal for 2025 is to expand to teach as many kids as possible from rural Tamil Nadu.

It takes many hearts and helping hands to run a program like this — tutors, coordinators, technology helpers, curriculum developers, and supporters. Whether you are a student looking to volunteer, a parent who wants to get involved, or a well-wisher who wants to support with resources, you are welcome to be part of this journey.

By partnering with us, you are not just helping teach English. You are connecting cultures, preserving roots, and empowering futures.

Ways to Help

Choose a role that fits your time, talent and passion.

📚

Become a Tutor

Teach a child English 1-on-1 through fun, structured online sessions and become their guide for life.

Your Role as a Tutor

Tutors are the heart and backbone of this program.

Prerequisites
  • Must be fluent in Tamil.
  • Passionate about helping kids learn and grow.
  • Ready to commit time and energy to making a difference.
Responsibilities
  • Conduct engaging English lessons based on our standardized curriculum for your student.
  • Use Tamil strategically to explain concepts and build rapport.
  • Encourage student participation and curiosity.
  • Ask for help when needed from the adult coordinator, especially for student tutors.
  • Adapt and refine teaching methodology to benefit students.
  • Go prepared. Before class, review the lesson content.
  • Keep track of time, flow, and lessons taught.
  • If this role interests you, there will be a follow-up orientation session to attend.

Time Commitment: Minimum of 1 to 1.5 hours per week, including tutoring, preparation for class, and any homework help offered. Any extra time offered is beneficial for the student.

🤝

Mentor / Coordinator

Help match tutors with students, schedule sessions, and ensure smooth communication on both sides.

Your Role as a Coordinator

Adult coordinators help student tutors run their sessions smoothly.

Prerequisites
  • Proficient in reading, writing, and speaking Tamil.
  • Familiar with Tamil culture and able to understand local customs and context so lessons stay respectful and relatable.
Responsibilities
  • Be present in all tutoring sessions with student tutors.
  • Troubleshoot technology and curriculum challenges during sessions.
  • Help smooth communication when needed with students, parents, and school staff.
  • Help tutors adapt when challenges arise.
  • Encourage and reassure students.
  • Provide emotional and logistical support to both tutor and student.
  • If this role interests you, there will be a follow-up orientation session to attend.

Time Commitment: Minimum of 1 hour per week per student. This can be split across multiple sessions.

📖

Curriculum Developer

Design lessons, worksheets and activities that make English engaging, practical and culturally relevant.

Your Role as a Curriculum Developer

Our curriculum has three parts: Basic Grammar, Cultural Exchange, and Practical Skill Building. If you can help with any or all of these, your contribution is welcome.

Responsibilities
  • Design tailored training materials and lesson plans based on skill level.
  • Incorporate cultural exchange moments in each session.
  • Share real-life examples and role-play situations to teach practical language use.
  • Match activities to the student’s English proficiency level.
  • Create bilingual English-Tamil teaching aids when needed.
  • Work with tutors to adapt lesson flow for cultural and practical relevance.
  • Update materials based on feedback from students, tutors, and adult coordinators.

Time Commitment: Variable and flexible based on what you can offer.

💻

Technology Implementor

Help build and maintain the tools that power our tutoring platform, website and student tracking systems.

Your Role as a Technology Implementor

Responsibilities
  • Assist in building and improving the website.
  • Create and maintain the YouTube channel and reels.
  • Support homework innovation by creating mobile-friendly assignments.
  • Help use AI and other emerging technologies to simplify lesson delivery and create engaging practice tools.

Time Commitment: Variable and flexible based on what you can offer.

Behind-the-Scene Helper

Support outreach, homework, scheduling, organization, and other behind-the-scenes program needs.

Be Part of Our Well-Oiled Machine Crew

Behind-the-scene helpers support important program operations.

Helpers may assist as
  • Story book readers
  • Homework helpers
  • Scheduling helpers who coordinate schedules with students and tutors
  • Google Drive organizers and maintainers

Time Commitment: Variable and flexible based on what you can offer.
